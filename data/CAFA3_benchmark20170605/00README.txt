File containing the benchmarks generated 2017-06-05 for initial
assessment of methods in the CAFA3 challenge. Results are preliminary
and non-binding.

For further information about CAFA3 and the terms used here, see: 
http://biofunctionprediction.org/cafa/

CAFA3 targets are available from http://biofunctionprediction.org/cafa/

For information about CAFA3 rules and terms used in here:
https://www.synapse.org/#!Synapse:syn5840147/wiki/402192


Benchmark IDs are taken from the CAFA target files, above.
Ontologies used: 

Gene Ontoloy (GO):
CCO: Cellular Component Ontology
MFO: Molecular Function Ontology
BPO: Biological Process Ontology

 
groundtruth/
Two-column files mapping target IDs to GO terms.
#1 CAFA3 target ID
#2 Leaf GO term

lists/
Benchmarks divided by species.
type1: No Knowledge (NK) benchmark protein
type2: Limited Knowledge (LK) benchmark protein
